About

Association for Computing Machinery

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, is a hub for tech enthusiasts, fostering innovation through seminars, discussions, and awareness of opportunities like Google Summer of Code (GSoC). By forming teams for hackathons, ACM cultivates technical excellence, leadership, and collaborative skills, driving technological advancements.
